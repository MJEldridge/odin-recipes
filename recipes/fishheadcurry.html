Singapore Fish Head Curry

A delicous preparation of Fish Head Curry, which is extremely popular in Singapore! Serve with steamed white rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized(about 500 grams) fish head
  • 1 large knob of ginger
  • 3 tbsp coriander leaves
  • A pinch of fenugreek seeds
  • 2 tbsp of curry leaves
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp garlic, chopped
  • 3 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 3 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/2 tsp fish curry masala powder(optional)
  • 1 tsp(or to taste) tamarind(Assam) pulp(soaked in 1 tbsp water)
  • 1 tomato chopped
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass, bruised
  • 1 knob(1 tbsp) of ginger, sliced into rounds
  • 5-6 laby fingers(tender ones), chopped
  • 2 small sized eggplants, quartered
  • 1-2 tbsp of long red chilli, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of thick coconut cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp of oil

Instructions:

  1. Firstly, rinse the fish head throughly.
  2. Using a mortar and pestle, crush the ginger well.
  3. Rub thr fish thoroughly with the crushed ginger, and a good sprinkling of salt.
  4. Sprinkle coriander leaves over the fish.
  5. Place a steamer rack(above boiling water) and steam covered for 15 minutes. Then set aside.
  6. Heat oil in a wide carbon steel wok OR an earthen pot.
  7. Add the fenugreek seeds and curry leaves. Allow to crackle.
  8. Add the chopped garlic and as soon as it's soft, add the chopped onion.
  9. Stir, and cook till the onions are soft and lightly golden - do not allow them to go brown.
  10. Now add all the spice powders.
  11. Stir and cook, adding a sprinkling of water so that the spices do not burn.
  12. Cook till the raw smell of the spices is gone.
  13. Add 2 1/2 cups of water.
  14. Bring to the boil.
  15. Add salt, tamarind solution, chopped tomato, lemongrass, and sliced ginger.
  16. Add the chopped okra to a hot pan with some oil and toss very briefly, just till they have turned bright green in color.
  17. When the curry has simmered briefly, add the chopped vegetables. Also, add the red chilli.
  18. Add the coconut cream and cook on low till simmered.
  19. Now gently add the steamed fish head. Coat the fish head well in the curry.
  20. Cover and cook on low heat for 5 - 8 minutes. Do not stir the curry or the fish head might come apart, instead swirl the pot when needed.
  21. After the curry as rested a while, you may choose to remove the bruised lemongrass - so that the aroma does not become to strong.
  22. When it's time to serve, bring to boil again. If using a earthen pot, it will continue to boil. Bring to the serving table while still simmering.
  23. You can also use a tabletop wax stove to serve the curry simmering.
  24. Serve with steamed white rice.
